How Does It Work?

Enter your property details and agency fees to get accurate yield net return estimates for the next three years.

  • 💸 Purchase Price: Total cost of the property.
  • 🏠 Monthly Rent: Expected rental income per month.
  • 📅 Agency Fees: Initial fee of 30% in the first year, 25% in the second, and 20% for subsequent years.

The calculation of rental yield is straightforward. Divide the annual rental income by the property's purchase price or current market value, then multiply by 100 to get a percentage. For example, if an individual purchases a property for £300,000 and it generates an annual rental income of £18,000, they can calculate the rental yield as 6% (£18,000 ÷ £300,000 x 100).
